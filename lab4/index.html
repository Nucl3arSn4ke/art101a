Making Margarita's

    Salt Ice Lime Simple Syrup Tequila Triple Sec Gran Marnier

    1 tablespoon of salt 9 oz Ice 2 Limes 2 oz Simple Syrup 2 oz Tequila 1 oz Triple Sec 1 oz Gran Marnier


Make Margarita -
    Salt rim of glass Add Ice to glass Squeeze 2 Limes into glass Add Simple Syrup Pour Tequila, Triple Sec, and Gran Marnier into glass Stir

    Enjoy!
